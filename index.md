# Privacy Policy for INSTA COUNTER

**Last updated:** May 30, 2026

**INSTA COUNTER** is a simple Discord bot that displays public Instagram follower counts.

### Information We Collect
- We do **not** collect, store, or track any personal data.
- The bot only fetches **publicly available** Instagram follower counts when requested by users.
- Temporary caching of public usernames/counts may be used for performance.

### Data Sharing
- We do not sell, rent, or share any user data with third parties.
- No personal information is transmitted or stored.

### Third-Party Services
- This bot uses Instagram’s public web data.
- It operates through Discord’s API.
- Hosted on standard cloud services.

### Contact Us
If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the bot owner through Discord.

---

This policy may be updated occasionally. Continued use of the bot means you accept the current policy.
